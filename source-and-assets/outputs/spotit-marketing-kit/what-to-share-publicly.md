# What To Share Publicly

## Safer To Share
- Brand-led images that show Spotit without revealing the exact workflow.
- Short captions about the problem: wound documentation, chronology, handover, and structured records.
- General product promise as a concept.
- Requests for feedback or pilot conversations.

## Keep Private For Now
- Full app screenshots showing every prompt.
- The exact assessment sequence.
- The body-map interaction details.
- Dressing/action option lists.
- Any measurement logic or automation ideas.
- Login details.
- Any real patient or NHS-identifiable data.

## Best Public Caption
Spotit is an early-stage wound-care documentation concept being developed to support clearer wound capture, structured assessment, dressing records, and clinical summaries. I am currently seeking feedback from clinicians, nurses, care providers, and potential pilot partners before clinical deployment.

## Protective Disclaimer
Spotit is not yet for live patient care, diagnosis, treatment decisions, or storage of real patient-identifiable data. Any pilot would require secure hosting, clinical governance, data protection review, consent process, and safety documentation.
