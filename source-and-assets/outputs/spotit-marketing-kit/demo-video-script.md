# Spotit Demo Video Script

## Length
45 to 60 seconds.

## Scene 1: Problem
Visual: clinician looking at notes, phone photos, and wound documentation.

Voiceover:
Wound documentation can become fragmented across notes, photos, dressing records, and handover messages.

## Scene 2: Spotit Concept
Visual: Spotit login and caseload.

Voiceover:
Spotit is an early-stage wound-care documentation concept built around a simple clinical workflow.

## Scene 3: Wound Flow
Visual: add patient, wound site, body map, camera capture.

Voiceover:
Clinicians can register a patient, select a wound site, capture a wound photo, and continue through structured assessment prompts.

## Scene 4: Assessment And Care
Visual: measurement, exudate, tissue type, dressing, mobility, repositioning, continence, nutrition, hydration.

Voiceover:
The concept supports wound measurements, tissue type, exudate, dressing choice, mobility, repositioning, continence, nutrition, hydration, and care-plan prompts.

## Scene 5: Summary
Visual: patient summary and report draft.

Voiceover:
The aim is to create a clearer chronological wound summary for review and handover.

## Closing Disclaimer
Visual: Spotit logo.

Voiceover:
Spotit is currently a demo concept seeking clinical feedback and pilot partners. It is not yet for live patient data or treatment decisions.
