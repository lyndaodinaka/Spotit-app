# Spotit Safe Marketing Copy

## Short Description
Spotit is an early-stage wound-care documentation concept designed to help clinicians and care teams capture wound photos, complete structured wound assessments, record dressing changes, and generate clearer wound summaries.

## Safe Disclaimer
Spotit is currently a concept/demo and is not yet intended for live patient care, diagnosis, treatment decisions, or storage of real patient-identifiable data. Any clinical pilot would require secure hosting, clinical governance, data protection review, consent processes, and appropriate safety documentation.

## One-Line Pitch
Spotit helps wound-care teams move from scattered notes and photos toward structured, chronological wound documentation.

## Longer Pitch
Spotit is being developed as a responsive wound-care documentation app for clinicians, nurses, and care providers. The concept supports patient registration, wound-site mapping, photo capture, wound assessment prompts, dressing records, mobility and repositioning prompts, and report drafting. We are seeking feedback and pilot conversations with clinicians and care organisations before clinical deployment.

## Avoid These Claims
- NHS approved
- Clinically validated
- Ready for real patient use
- Diagnoses wounds
- Guaranteed automatic wound measurement
- Replaces clinician judgement
- Stores patient data securely, unless the hosted secure version has been completed and tested

## Safer Call To Action
Interested in helping shape Spotit? We are inviting clinicians, nurses, care homes, and wound-care providers to review the demo and discuss pilot requirements.
