# Where To Market Spotit Safely

## Best Places To Start
- LinkedIn: post the concept and ask for clinician feedback.
- Local care-home groups: ask managers what they need from wound documentation.
- Community nursing contacts: request informal feedback, not clinical use.
- Tissue viability nurse networks: ask what workflow problems Spotit should solve.
- Care conferences and local health innovation events: present as an early-stage concept.
- Private wound-care providers: ask about pilot interest once governance is ready.

## Suggested First Post

I am currently seeking feedback from clinicians, care homes, and wound-care teams before any clinical deployment. This is not for live patient use yet; I am looking for workflow feedback and potential pilot conversations.
