Spotit Wound Care

Spotit Wound Care is a digital wound-care workflow app founded by Lynda Chidi to support wound capture, assessment workflow, review planning, caseload visibility and reporting.

Open Spotit | Privacy | Data deletion

What Spotit Is About

Spotit is designed to help healthcare teams organise wound-care workflow in one place. It focuses on structured wound information, review planning, patient/caseload visibility and cleaner reporting conversations.

  • Wound capture workflow
  • Assessment and care-planning prompts
  • Review status and caseload visibility
  • Reporting and MDT sharing workflow concepts
  • Restricted access for approved users

Founder

Spotit Wound Care was founded by Lynda Chidi through Medholic as a practical wound-care workflow idea for controlled buyer review, organisation demonstrations and future pilot discussions.

Contact: lynda.chidi@medholic.net

Current Stage

Spotit is currently positioned for private demonstration, buyer review and controlled testing. Dashboard access is restricted and granted by approval only.

The app is not for entering real patient data unless a deploying organisation completes its governance, clinical safety and regulatory checks.

Stages Left Before Live Clinical Use

  • GDPR review and DPIA
  • Data Processing Agreement
  • Information governance and security review
  • Clinical safety case and hazard review
  • Medical-device/regulatory review where applicable
  • Controlled pilot sign-off by the deploying organisation
Important: Spotit supports workflow and documentation. It does not replace professional clinical judgement, local escalation procedures, safeguarding duties or regulated clinical review.
