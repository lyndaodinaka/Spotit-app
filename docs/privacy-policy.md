# Spotit Privacy Policy

Copyright (c) 2026 Spotit. All rights reserved on behalf of the Spotit owner.

Spotit is designed to support wound-care documentation, wound photography, measurement, care planning, report generation and MDT communication.

This application may process patient identifiers, demographics, clinical history, wound photographs, wound measurements, care plans, reports, consent records and audit logs.

Real patient data must only be used after Spotit is deployed to a secure cloud environment with authenticated access, role permissions, HTTPS, private encrypted image storage, database backups, audit logging, DPIA completion, clinical safety review and appropriate data processing agreements.

Patients should be informed why wound photographs and measurements are recorded, who may access reports, how MDT sharing works, and how consent can be reviewed or withdrawn according to local policy.
