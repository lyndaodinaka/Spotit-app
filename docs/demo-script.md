# Spotit Demo Script

## Opening
Spotit is a wound-care documentation app for clinicians and nurses. It helps teams capture wound photos, complete structured assessments, record dressing care, and create a clear report.

## Demo Flow
1. Log in as a clinician.
2. Search the caseload.
3. Add a fresh patient using ID, date of birth, history, allergy, diabetes, and consent details.
4. Add wound history, referral date, onset date, and wound type.
5. Select the wound site on the body map.
6. Capture the wound photo.
7. Complete measurement and assessment prompts.
8. Record primary dressing, secondary dressing, solution used, and actions required.
9. Complete mobility, repositioning, continence, stool, hygiene, nutrition, hydration, and assistive aid prompts.
10. Add final clinical notes and finish.
11. Show the healing trend, escalation prompt, patient summary, and report sending options.

## Buyer Questions To Ask
- How do you currently record wound measurements and photos?
- How long does a dressing-change record take?
- What gets missed most often?
- Who needs the final report?
- What would make this safe enough for a pilot in your service?
