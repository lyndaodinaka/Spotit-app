# Spotit Google Play Listing Draft

## App Name

Spotit Wound Care

## Short Description

Capture wounds, assess progress, plan reviews, and prepare wound-care reports.

## Full Description

Spotit is a wound-care workflow app designed to help clinicians organise wound capture, assessment, review planning, caseload visibility, and reporting in one place.

The app supports a structured wound-care flow for demonstration, service planning, and buyer discussions. It is designed for Android phones and tablets, including Samsung, Redmi/Xiaomi, and other Google Play compatible devices.

Spotit can help teams present a clearer wound-care process, track review priorities, and prepare cleaner reporting conversations.

## Target Devices

- Android phones
- Android tablets
- Samsung devices
- Redmi/Xiaomi devices
- Other Google Play compatible devices

## Category

Medical

## Audience

Healthcare services, wound-care teams, care providers, managers, buyers, and investors.

## Demo Safety Statement

This version should be marketed as a demonstration or workflow-support app unless an organisation has completed its own privacy, information governance, clinical safety, and patient-data approval checks.

## Privacy Summary

Spotit should not expose private admin credentials, database secrets, real patient details, or internal security keys in public marketing material.
