# Spotit Build Next Steps

## What Exists Now
- A clickable Spotit demo screen in `outputs/wound-care-prototype.html`.
- A production backend starter with secure login, patient, wound, assessment, care plan, report, audit, and escalation foundations.
- Clinical safety, data protection, pilot, and API documents.

## What Must Be Built Before Selling
1. Responsive frontend app connected to the backend.
2. Camera capture that opens the device camera and stores wound photos privately.
3. Admin area for creating clinicians and assigning roles.
4. Patient timeline showing every wound capture by date and time.
5. Report sending with consent checks and audit trail.
6. Role permissions for admin, clinician, nurse, nurse assistant, and support worker.
7. Hosting in a UK/EU environment suitable for health data.
8. Clinical safety review, DPIA, privacy notice, terms, and support process.

## Pilot Version
The pilot should be limited to a small group, with test patients or explicit approved clinical governance. It should prove:
- Clinicians can register patients quickly.
- Wound capture and assessment are easier than paper.
- Reports are clear enough for handover.
- Escalation prompts are helpful and not noisy.
- The data export satisfies the organisation.

## Paid Version
The paid version needs:
- Subscription or licence model.
- Support email and response times.
- Backups and recovery process.
- Training material.
- Security review.
- Clear contract and data-processing terms.
