## Short Description

Capture wounds, assess progress, plan reviews, and prepare wound-care reports.

## Full Description

Spotit is a wound-care workflow app designed to help clinicians and wound-care teams organise wound capture, assessment, review planning, caseload visibility, and reporting in one place.

Spotit supports a structured demonstration workflow for wound documentation, priority visibility, review planning, and reporting conversations. It is designed for Android phones and tablets, including Samsung, Redmi/Xiaomi, and other Google Play compatible devices.

This version should be treated as a demonstration and workflow-support app unless an organisation has completed its own privacy, information governance, clinical safety, and patient-data approval checks.

## App Access Instructions For Google Reviewers

Spotit uses restricted login. Please use the temporary reviewer login provided in the App access section of Play Console. The login is for Google review only and should not be shared publicly.

## Ads

Choose `No` unless adverts are added later.

## Target Audience

Choose adult/professional users. Do not choose children as the target audience.

## Data Safety Starting Point

Complete this according to the actual approved release. If the app is submitted as a demo with restricted access, state only what the app actually collects and transmits in that release. If real patient data is enabled later, update the Data safety form before release.

## Medical/Clinical Positioning

Do not describe Spotit as replacing a clinician. Use wording such as:

`Spotit supports wound-care workflow, documentation, review planning, and reporting. It does not replace professional clinical judgement or local escalation procedures.`

## Reviewer Login Safety

Create a temporary reviewer account. Do not give Google your private owner/admin password.
