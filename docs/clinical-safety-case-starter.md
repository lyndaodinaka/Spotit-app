# Spotit Clinical Safety Case Starter

This document is a starter and does not replace formal clinical safety review.

## Intended Use

Spotit supports wound-care documentation, workflow visibility, review planning and reporting.

## Not Intended For

Spotit is not intended to replace:

- Professional clinical judgement
- Diagnosis
- Prescribing decisions
- Emergency escalation
- Local clinical policy
- Regulated clinical review

## Main Clinical Risks

- Incorrect wound data entered
- Wrong patient selected
- Wound photo linked to wrong record
- Review date missed
- Deterioration not escalated
- Report sent to wrong recipient
- User relies on app instead of clinical judgement

## Existing Controls

- Restricted login
- Patient summary view
- Consent prompts
- Audit logging foundation
- Status indicators
- Review planning fields
- Report action logging
- Deletion reason prompts

## Required Controls Before Live Use

- Clinical safety officer review
- Hazard log
- Local SOPs
- User training
- Escalation policy
- Pilot feedback review
- Clinical incident process
- Report recipient validation
- Real-world testing with approved data only

## Pilot Safety Questions

- Does the workflow reduce missed reviews?
- Are users able to identify deteriorating wounds clearly?
- Are reports understandable?
- Are review dates and deferrals handled safely?
- Are patient records easy to distinguish?
- Are consent and photo capture prompts clear?
