# Spotit Regulatory Intended Use Review

## Purpose

This document helps keep Spotit positioned safely and supports discussion with regulatory advisers.

## Current Intended Use Statement

Spotit Wound Care supports wound-care documentation, wound photo workflow, assessment recording, review planning, caseload visibility and reporting.

## Current Positioning

Spotit is positioned as a workflow-support and documentation tool for private demonstration, buyer review and controlled testing.

## Avoid These Claims Unless Formally Reviewed

Do not claim that Spotit:

- Diagnoses wounds
- Determines treatment
- Replaces clinical judgement
- Automatically detects infection
- Predicts healing outcomes for clinical decision-making
- Makes autonomous care decisions
- Replaces clinician review

## Regulatory Review Trigger Points

Seek regulatory advice before adding or marketing:

- Automated diagnosis
- Treatment recommendations
- Clinical decision support
- AI wound classification
- Automated infection detection
- Automated risk scoring used for clinical action
- Integration that directly changes clinical records without review

## Buyer Statement

Spotit should be reviewed by the deploying organisation to confirm whether its intended use, configuration and local workflow create any medical device or regulatory obligations.
