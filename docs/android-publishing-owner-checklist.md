# Spotit Android Publishing Owner Checklist

These are the owner-only steps needed before Spotit can appear on Google Play.

## Google Play Console

1. Create or open a Google Play Console developer account.
2. Pay the Google Play developer registration fee.
3. Complete identity verification.
4. If using a new personal developer account, complete Google's required app testing steps before public release.

## App Setup

1. Create a new app named `Spotit Wound Care`.
2. Choose app type: App.
3. Choose category: Medical.
4. Choose free or paid.
5. Add contact email: `lynda.chidi@medholic.net`.
6. Add privacy policy URL: `https://spotit-app-production-6d30.up.railway.app/privacy.html`.
7. Add terms URL where requested: `https://spotit-app-production-6d30.up.railway.app/terms.html`.

## App Content Forms

1. Privacy policy.
2. Ads: No, unless advertising is later added.
3. App access/sign-in details: provide Google reviewers with a temporary reviewer login.
4. Target audience: adults/professionals, not children.
5. Data safety: answer according to the approved production data handling.
6. Content rating questionnaire.
7. Health/medical declarations if requested by Google.

## Release Pack Needed

1. Android App Bundle file: `.aab`.
2. App icon.
3. Feature graphic.
4. Phone screenshots.
5. Tablet screenshots.
6. Short description.
7. Full description.
8. Privacy policy URL.
9. Reviewer login instructions.

## Important Safety Rule

Do not submit private admin credentials in a document or public app listing. Create a temporary reviewer login inside the app admin area and disable it after review if needed.
