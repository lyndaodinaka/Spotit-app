# Spotit

## Wound assessment, capture, and reporting for clinical teams

Spotit helps nurses and clinicians record wound progress in a structured, chronological way across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

## Core Value
- Faster wound assessment workflow.
- Photo capture linked to body site, date, and time.
- Chronological active wound summary.
- Structured prompts for wound type, measurement, exudate, tissue type, pain, dressing, mobility, continence, nutrition, hydration, and escalation.
- Report generation for clinical handover.
- Audit trail for safer governance.

## Who It Helps
- Community nursing teams.
- Care homes.
- Tissue viability services.
- Private wound-care providers.
- Clinics managing repeat dressing changes.

## Why It Is Different
Spotit follows the clinician's natural flow: register the patient, capture the wound, assess the wound, document the care plan, then generate a summary and escalation view.

## Pilot Offer
A pilot can be offered to a small team to test:
- Time saved per dressing change.
- Completeness of wound records.
- Consistency of wound assessment.
- Quality of clinical handover.
- Usability across phones, tablets, and laptops.
