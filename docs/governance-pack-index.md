## Current Position

Spotit is suitable for private demonstration, buyer review and controlled testing. Real patient data must not be entered unless the deploying organisation completes its own GDPR, DPIA, information governance, clinical safety, security, regulatory and local approval checks.

## Recommended Buyer Workflow

1. Review Spotit demo using fake/test data.
2. Confirm intended use and whether any medical device review is needed.
3. Complete DPIA and information governance review.
4. Review security, hosting, access controls and retention plan.
5. Review clinical safety and local SOPs.
6. Agree pilot scope and responsibilities.
7. Sign Data Processing Agreement if Spotit/Medholic processes data on behalf of the organisation.
8. Begin controlled pilot only after written approval.
